Indelicates 2020
Graduate Collection

My 2020 Honours Research project, ‘Indelicates’ allowed me to gain a better understanding of the pre-existing cultural foundations of gendered dress, with a particular focus on Femininity. The use of disassembled and preloved female undergarments allowed the collection to intentionally subvert traditionally feminine aesthetics, detailing and archetypes. This section shows a variety of physical outcomes and imagery that provoke thought towards genders influence on dress.
