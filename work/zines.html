Feminine Feelings

As a part of a Mini project workshop for my Honours year, I produced the Zine ‘Feminine Feelings’. This Zine allowed me to explore the purpose and practical use of this visual medium to communicate ideas and attitudes towards modern dress and experiences of gender.

Through a collaboration with female friends and family, I collated a reflection of modern-day interpretations of femininity and feminine culture, using various forms of visual media such as calligrams, illustration and collage in a monochromatic colour palette.

I enjoyed this as a way of communicating my work and plan to explore it further in my future practice, post-graduation.
