Melbourne, AUS Graduate Designer

Hi, my name is Grace Dawson. I am currently completing my Bachelor of Fashion (Design) (Honours) course at RMIT University in Melbourne.

My practice and past work have focused on the more conceptual side to fashion and design, interested by the potential influence of engaging in thought-provoking fashion outcomes. The research area I specialise in predominantly focuses on gender and its engagement with fashion and dress.

My experience includes digital media, graphic design, material manipulation and garment construction.
